MaitHacks

Hack it till you Crack it!

8 Hours of pitching on provided themes collaborating to code, develop and deploy innovative solutions!

MaitHacks    

To check your innovative mind and coding skills grab the opportunity of HackMait. The event consists of two rounds, starting from 13th April at 10 am for the next 36 hours. Participants need to register in teams of maximum 4.

ROUND 1

It will be an online round where participants will get two random keywords generated from the website on 7th April from 9 pm onwards. The teams would be required to submit their project ideas based on both of the keywords provided and upload on our website before 12 pm of 8th April 2018. The results of Round 1 will be sent to the shortlisted teams via email on the same day by 6 pm. Selection will be based on the originality and usefulness of the idea submitted.

ROUND 2

The Second Round will be held in the MAIT Campus on 9th April - 9am onwards. In this round, selected teams from round 1 would have to come for the project pitching and demonstration.

 

Get ready for prizes worth Rs 1 lakh, Cash prize of Rs 20,000 , internship and incubation offers, Certificates, .tech domains to everyone and much more. Come on guys, do it for the cookies, if nothing else.
